Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services and booking process.

Booking a session is easy! Simply fill out the quote form above with your details and preferred package. I'll get back to you within 24-48 hours to discuss availability and next steps. A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking date.

I recommend bringing 2-3 outfit options that reflect your personal style and the look you want to achieve. Solid colors generally work best, and it's good to avoid busy patterns or logos that can be distracting. I'll provide a detailed style guide after booking with more specific recommendations based on your session type and location.

The delivery time depends on the package you choose. For Basic packages, you'll receive your photos within 7 business days. Standard packages are delivered within 5 business days, and Elite packages have a 3-business day turnaround. I always aim to deliver your images as quickly as possible without compromising on quality.

All packages include high-resolution digital images delivered through a private online gallery where you can download your photos. The Elite package includes one 8x10" print. I also offer professional print services and custom albums as add-ons. You'll have the option to purchase additional prints and products directly through your gallery.

I understand that plans can change. If you need to reschedule, please provide at least 48 hours' notice, and your deposit can be applied to a new date within 3 months. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the session will result in forfeiture of the deposit. In case of inclement weather, we can reschedule at no additional cost.
